Food

Jamaican food is known for bold flavors and spices. Popular dishes include jerk chicken, curry goat, oxtail, and the national dish: ackee and saltfish. Other favorites include festivals, patties, bammy, and rice and peas.

Music

Jamaica is the birthplace of reggae music, made famous by Bob Marley, Peter Tosh, and Jimmy Cliff. Other genres such as ska, dancehall, and rocksteady also originated on the island.

Traditions

Jamaicans celebrate events such as Independence Day, Christmas, Easter, Carnival, Emancipation Day, and Reggae Month. The popular greeting “Wah Gwaan” reflects the warm and friendly nature of the people.
